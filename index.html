I'm Nevin McIntyre, a GIS enthusiast working remotely for 3-GIS in northern Idaho. I have a strong background in implementing GIS for government organizations using ESRI software and solutions. I enjoy learning and utilizing open source GIS technologies on personal projects - Python, Mapbox, Geopandas, Leaflet, QGIS, OSM and more!

In my free time I develop web applications, create/maintain open data, and spatial data visualizations. My projects are solely for educational purposes. However, I try to conduct analysis which might be useful. I'm also a guitarist and enjoy writing music using Reaper. View my resume on Linkden and lets connect!



If you've enjoyed, used or were inspired by my work and would like to send a thank you: I would be extremely grateful!
