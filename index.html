I'm Nevin McIntyre, a GIS Analyst/enthusiast working remotely for Sidwell in Northern Idaho. I have a strong background with implementing GIS for government organizations using ESRI software and solutions. I enjoy learning and utilizing open source GIS technologies on personal projects - Python, Geopandas, Leaflet, QGIS, OSM and more!

In my free time I develop web applications, create/maintain open data, and create maps & spatial data visualizations. These projects are for fun and educational development. However, I try to find data and analysis that might be useful. I'm also a guitarist and enjoy writing music using Reaper and VST Plug-ins.

If you've enjoyed, used or were inspired by my work and would like to send a thank you: I would be extremely grateful!

Feel free to reach out!
